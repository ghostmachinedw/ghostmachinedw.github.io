Dark Woods

A Ghost Machine PRODUCTION

A strange forest beyond the Sun’s reach. How did you end up here? And can you find a way out? As you explore beneath the seemingly endless black canopies, you hear something move from behind you. Enter Dark Woods, the place beyond the place beyond the pines. Swipe vigorously from hallway to hallway, as you solve the puzzling path and escape the dark entity that stalks these trees. Who knows? Maybe you might outlast your friends, and by you, we mean your high score. Available for iOS and Android.
